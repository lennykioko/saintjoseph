Congratulations! You made it! Today, you will consecrate yourself to St. Joseph. The Holy Trinity wants St. Joseph to be more known and loved. You have been invited to imitate the virtues and holiness of St. Joseph's pure heart. With St. Joseph at your side, virtue and holiness will increase in your life. With St. Joseph's paternal cloak over you, you will be protected from spiritual harm. Fear nothing! Your spiritual father is the father of Jesus, the husband of the Mother of God, and the Terror of Demons.<FootnoteRef id="xxiv" />
